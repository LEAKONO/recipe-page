üçó Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch for 15 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or pan.
  3. Stir fry garlic, then add chicken.
  4. Add sliced veggies, cook until tender-crisp.
  5. Serve hot over rice or noodles.

üçî Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Form the ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or skillet over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a pan.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patty on the bottom bun and adding lettuce, tomato, pickles, ketchup, and mustard.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun and serve.
