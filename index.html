🍝 Spaghetti Bolognese

  • 1000g ground beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • salt and paper

Instructions

🧀 Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 200g macaroni
  • 1½ cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil macaroni and drain.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and stir in flour.
  3. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly.
  4. Add cheese and stir until melted.
  5. Mix in macaroni and season to taste.
